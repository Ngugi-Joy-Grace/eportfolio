Research Proposal Presentation Outline

This activity presented an opportunity to develop an outline for the research proposal presentation on a topic similar to my literature review. The feedback on the outline indicated that it was focused and concise, allowing for an in-depth discussion. However, a caution was given to state if the topic was similar to the intended dissertation topic. Since my dissertation topic is within the scope of my research proposal, I opted to be cautious and drop it altogether, choosing a new topic: "Sentiment Analysis in a Multilingual Context with a Focus on Swahili."
