Research Methods & Professional Practices

Professional Skills matrix, SWOT Analysis, Action Plan

These three pieces are essential to tracking my skills progression through the course. Of particular importance to this module are research, academic writing, time management, presentation and communication, and many others indicated in the pieces.

My research and academic writing were extensively tested, especially with the two main assignments: the literature review and the research proposal presentation. These assignments created a cycle of learning, relearning, and unlearning for me which I discuss in my reflective piece.

As for time management, as indicated in my SWOT analysis, working full-time is a deterrent to me fully focusing on the course. This worked fine with other modules, but for this module, which required 100% effort, I felt the effects health-wise, often battling fatigue and completing assignments at the last minute. This led to me scoring merits, which is not the norm for me.
