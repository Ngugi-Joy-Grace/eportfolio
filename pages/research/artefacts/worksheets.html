Research Methods & Professional Practices

Worksheets

These activities significantly improved my skills in statistical analysis with Microsoft Excel, particularly in hypothesis testing (t-tests) and data visualisation with bar charts and histograms. Since I completed the assignments later in the module, I did not experience the errors in the material that some of my peers did, as they had already been fixed.

I would like to practice these statistical analysis skills with spreadsheets on real-world projects. It would be interesting to use spreadsheets as opposed to Python, which I use on a daily basis. However, I note this with caution, as using spreadsheets with large datasets may present issues such as performance slowdowns, limited data capacity, and potential errors in data handling (Smith et.al, 2022).
