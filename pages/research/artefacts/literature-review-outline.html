Research Methods & Professional Practices

Implementing Deep Learning Tools and Techniques in Customer Profiling.

This outline focused on my literature review topic and identified the research gap in ‘Implementing Deep Learning Tools and Techniques in Customer Profiling.’ The feedback I received on my literature review outline was invaluable in helping me refine my approach. I initially included numerous sub-sections to cover various aspects of deep learning. However, this breadth would dilute the depth of analysis required within the 2,000-word limit. Taking the suggestion to focus on a core discussion and critically explore it, I chose a more focused topic: ‘Enhancing E-commerce Customer Profiling through Multi-modal Data Integration Using Deep Learning.’ This approach aligns with Johnson's (2023) recommendation for structuring literature reviews for greater impact.
