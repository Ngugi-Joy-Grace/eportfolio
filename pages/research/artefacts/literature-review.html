Research Methods & Professional Practices

Enhancing E-commerce Customer Profiling through Multi-modal Data Integration Using Deep Learning

The feedback indicated that there was some very good knowledge demonstrated in the content, and that there was a diverse research mix. However, my review seemed unfocused and shifted between different points. Additionally, the 'silo' discussion approach I used detracted from creating a more cohesive discussion. Essentially, this means I needed to integrate my paragraphs with direct comparisons throughout, allowing for a critical context.

The feedback also pointed out that the Turnitin report exposed a few matching phrases. I realise that if I had submitted earlier to the Turnitin sandbox, I would have spotted these matches and corrected them.

Overall, this assignment and feedback contributed significantly to improving my academic writing and research skills. Particularly, the feedback highlighted the importance of structuring my critical discussion from a 'silo' approach to a more integrated and comparative analysis.
