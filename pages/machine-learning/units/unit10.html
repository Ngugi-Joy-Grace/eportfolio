Visualisations

  1. Convolution layers Activation maps

    • Layer 1 (Conv2D): Displays the feature maps generated by the first convolutional layer, highlighting edges and simple textures.
    • Layer 2 (Conv2D): Shows more complex patterns and shapes as the network progresses through the layers.

    • Layer 4 (Conv2D): Further abstraction, capturing sophisticated image structures.

    • Layer 7 (Dense): Final dense layer before the output, summarising the learned features.
